Majlis
Luxury Guest Wing

Project Overview

The Luxury Guest Wing is a premier hospitality suite designed for exclusive events and high-end privacy. It features a Grand Private Majlis with a capacity for over 100 guests, making it ideal for weddings and private functions.

The wing also includes two luxury bedrooms for overnight stay, a spacious professional kitchen for large-scale catering, and a grand dining hall for formal banquets.
